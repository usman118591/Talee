WHAT IS TALEE ?

Talee is a service for businesses that needs in-field idea validation and design promotion. Talee links businesses with people who act as independent validators for collecting and analyzing big data. It also helps the validators (common people) to gain insights in the respective field and earn some pocket money at the same time.
